# University Logos Carousel - Dark Mode Styling Options

Here are several options that will blend seamlessly with your dark mode design:

## Option 1: Subtle Muted Logos (Recommended)
**Effect**: Logos are visible but muted, blending naturally with dark background
- Lower opacity (40-50%)
- Slightly desaturated white/gray
- Subtle hover effect
- **Best for**: Minimal, professional look

## Option 2: Subtle Background Container
**Effect**: Very subtle dark background behind logos for definition
- Dark background: `bg-slate-900/20` or `bg-slate-800/10`
- Logos at 60-70% opacity
- Subtle border: `border-slate-700/20`
- **Best for**: Slight separation without popping out

## Option 3: Gradient Overlay Effect
**Effect**: Subtle gradient overlay on logos for depth
- Logos with gradient mask overlay
- Blue/orange tint matching your accent colors
- Very subtle (10-15% opacity)
- **Best for**: Adding depth while staying subtle

## Option 4: Minimal Border Accent
**Effect**: Just a subtle border around each logo container
- Thin border: `border border-slate-700/30`
- Rounded corners
- Logos at 50% opacity
- **Best for**: Clean, defined look

## Option 5: Glow Effect (Subtle)
**Effect**: Very subtle glow around logos
- Soft shadow: `shadow-lg shadow-blue-500/5`
- Logos at 60% opacity
- Hover: slight glow increase
- **Best for**: Adding interest without being distracting

## Option 6: Dark Card Style
**Effect**: Logos in subtle dark cards matching your site's card style
- Background: `bg-slate-800/30`
- Border: `border border-slate-700/40`
- Rounded: `rounded-lg`
- Padding around logos
- **Best for**: Consistent with your card-based design

---

**My Recommendation**: Option 1 (Subtle Muted Logos) or Option 2 (Subtle Background Container) - these will blend best with your dark mode aesthetic without standing out too much.
