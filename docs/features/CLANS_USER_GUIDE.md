# Clans & Classrooms - User Guide

## What Are Clans?

Clans are private groups (like classrooms or study groups) where members can:
- Compete on private leaderboards
- Participate in clan-wide quiz sessions
- Track progress together
- See member stats and rankings
- Collaborate on learning goals

## Getting Started

### For Teachers (Pro Account Required)

#### Step 1: Upgrade to Pro
1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click "Upgrade to Pro" or visit `/pricing`
3. Subscribe to Pro ($4.99/month)

#### Step 2: Create Your Clan
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí **Clans & Classrooms** section
2. Click **"Create Clan"** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Clan Name**: e.g., "AP Biology 2024" or "Period 1 Math"
   - **Description** (optional): Brief description of the class/group
   - **Max Members**: 1-100 (default: 50)
   - **Private Clan**: Keep checked (requires join code)
4. Click **"Create Clan"**
5. You'll receive an **8-character join code** (e.g., "ABC123XY")

#### Step 3: Share the Join Code
- Share the code with your students via:
  - Email
  - Classroom announcement
  - Learning management system
  - In-person instruction

#### Step 4: Host Quiz Sessions
- Create quiz sessions for your clan
- All members can participate
- Track results on the clan leaderboard

### For Students (Free Account Works!)

#### Step 1: Get the Join Code
- Receive the 8-character code from your teacher/organizer

#### Step 2: Join the Clan
1. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí **Clans & Classrooms** section
2. Click **"Join Clan"** button
3. Enter the 8-character code (case-insensitive)
4. Click **"Join Clan"**

#### Step 3: Participate
- View clan details and members
- Check the leaderboard
- Join clan quiz sessions when hosted
- Track your progress

## Managing Your Clans

### As an Owner

- **View Clan Details**: Click on any clan card
- **Copy Join Code**: Click the copy button next to the code
- **View Members**: See all members and their roles
- **View Leaderboard**: Track member progress
- **Host Sessions**: Create quiz sessions for the clan (coming soon)
- **Delete Clan**: Remove the clan entirely (coming soon)

### As a Member

- **View Clan Details**: Click on any clan card
- **Copy Join Code**: Share with friends (if allowed)
- **View Members**: See all members
- **View Leaderboard**: See your ranking
- **Leave Clan**: Click "Leave Clan" button (if you're not the owner)

## Troubleshooting

### "Creating clans requires a Pro account"
- **Solution**: Upgrade to Pro at `/pricing`
- **Note**: Only creators need Pro - members can join for free!

### "You can only join up to 3 clans"
- **Solution**: Leave a clan you're no longer using, or upgrade to Pro to join up to 10 clans

### "Clan not found"
- **Solution**: Double-check the join code (8 characters, case-insensitive)
- Make sure the code hasn't expired or been changed

### "This clan is full"
- **Solution**: Contact the clan owner to increase the member limit or wait for a spot

### "You are already a member"
- **Solution**: You're already in this clan - check your clan list

## Tips for Teachers

1. **Use Descriptive Names**: Make clan names clear (e.g., "AP Bio Period 1" not "Bio1")
2. **Set Appropriate Limits**: 50 members is usually enough for a class
3. **Keep Codes Private**: Only share with intended members
4. **Regular Sessions**: Host quiz sessions regularly to keep engagement high
5. **Monitor Progress**: Check the leaderboard to see who needs extra help

## Tips for Students

1. **Join Multiple Clans**: Free users can join up to 3 clans (great for multiple classes)
2. **Check Leaderboard**: See how you rank and set goals
3. **Participate Actively**: Join clan sessions to improve your ranking
4. **Track Progress**: Monitor your stats to see improvement over time
