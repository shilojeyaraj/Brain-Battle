# Brain Battle Logo Generation Prompt

Create a modern, playful logo for "Brain Battle" - an AI-powered multiplayer study platform where students compete in real-time quiz battles.

**Brand Identity:**
- Name: "Brain Battle" (stylized as "BrainBattle" or "Brain Battle")
- Personality: Energetic, competitive, educational, fun, modern
- Audience: High school and college students

**Visual Style:**
- Cartoon/playful aesthetic with bold, chunky shapes
- Vibrant colors (primary blues, electric yellow/orange accents)
- Bold, readable typography
- Modern flat design with subtle depth
- Works on light and dark backgrounds

**Key Elements:**
- Stylized brain icon (gaming/tech style, not anatomical)
- Competitive elements (lightning bolts, trophy, or battle symbols)
- Subtle AI/tech motifs (circuit patterns, digital particles)
- Dynamic composition suggesting speed and competition

**Variations Needed:**
1. Full logo: icon + text (horizontal)
2. Icon-only mark (for favicons/app icons)
3. Stacked version (icon above text)
4. Wordmark-only (text without icon)

**Technical:**
- Scalable vector design
- Works from 16x16px to large banners
- High contrast for accessibility
- Simple and memorable

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Vibrant blue (#3B82F6)
- Accent: Electric yellow/orange (#F59E0B)
- Secondary: Purple/cyan for tech feel

**Style Reference:** Gaming logos (Fortnite, Among Us) meets educational platforms (Duolingo, Khan Academy) with competitive energy. Should make studying feel exciting and social.
